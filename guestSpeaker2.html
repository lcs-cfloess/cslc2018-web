Carlo Bos

Co-President, CTI

As Co-President of CTI, a globally recognized Leadership Development and Coach Training Company, Carlo is responsible for the co-leadership and stewardship of CTI’s mission, vision, and strategy as well as the health of its people, culture and finances. Founded in 1992, CTI is the home of Co-Active® Leadership and Co-Active® Coaching, one of the most renowned and utilized approaches to coach training in the world.

Carlo holds degrees in both Economics and Education/Psychology and is a proud product of CTI’s leadership development programs. He is a fierce stand for CTI’s Point of View (POV) on leadership, which is helping to revolutionize the language and concept of leadership. CTI’s POV on leadership is:

Everyone is a leader. We create our world. Together. Everyday.

Possessing an adventurous spirit and an unending yearning to be a part of powerful team’s, Carlo has explored and practiced leadership in many arenas around the world. He has run a hostel in Australia, lead major capital campaigns for non-profits across Canada, served as Global Faculty for two tier one leadership development organizations (Duke University and CTI), and was a key player in the establishment of one of Canada’s first integrated Leadership Development practices at Knightsbridge Human Capital Solutions. He has also tried his hand as an entrepreneur, running his own Leadership Development Company for close to five years. Over his 15+ years working in Leadership Development, he has partnered with teams and leaders in over 20 countries and for many recognizable brands. His life long fascination with human development, potential and consciousness is at the core of what inspires him to dig deep into this work and share it around the world.

Carlo is a graduate of Lakefield College School (1994), has served as an LCS Trustee since 2011 and was on the School Board from 2013-2016.
