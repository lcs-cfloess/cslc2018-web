Dr. Betsy McGregor




Harvard Medical School Research Fellow on Ethics in Science, Harvard Kennedy School Fellow on Mountains & Leadership, Doctor of Veterinary Medicine, Ontario Veterinary College, Climbed 2 of the world's Seven Summits in Argentina and Africa, Walked El Camino in Spain, United Nations Young Professional in Indonesia and Europe, Mother of former European pro-hockey player, leadership instructor at Lakefield College School and SHAD Fellows on campuses across Canada, UNESCO Author, Women in Science, World Science Report, and currently writing a book "On Ramp" - highlights from inspiring women in leadership in Canada.
